Providing the quality service you can count on for over 20 years

Jim, Rick and Pam were committed to providing the kind of service they would approve of for themselves when starting Young & Sons Heating and Air Conditioning in 1991. Developing a small-town approach using value-added core principles of personalized service, professionalism and a dedication to customer satisfaction they went on to established an exceptional reputation for heating and air conditioning companies in the region that remains unmatched.

With one phone call to 651-433-4500 home owners, businesses and contractors alike experience Young & Sons dedicated team of experts for new construction, renovations, and restorations to bring units up to building codes for error-free installations and repairs.

Their goal to improve your home or business with worry-free 'total indoor comfort' using quality manufactured furnace units, boilers, AC appliances and parts in perfect working order has been realized time and again. By providing service overhauls, furnace and air conditioning installations and much more, Young & Sons Heating and Air Conditioning has earned the appreciation and trust from customers throughout the St. Croix River Valley area in Stillwater, Forest Lake, Scandia, Hugo, Mahtomedi, White Bear Lake, Bayport, Lakeland, Oak Park Heights, Afton, Lake Elmo, and Marine on St. Croix.

Due to their efforts and hard work, in 2000, Young & Sons received the top rating from American Standard reaching the elite level of 'Customer Care Dealer' as reserved for only the finest heating and air conditioning professionals in the business. Read a few testimonials from happy customers

In addition to achieving top ranking from American Standard for their impeccable satisfied customer record, Young & Sons Heating and Air Conditioning is also recognized for securing an A+ Rating from the Better Business Bureau for maintaining a zero complaint performance

The specialists at Young & Sons Heating and Air Conditioning consult you in the most efficient and effective heating and cooling solutions for new construction. Mindful of budgets, architectural designed layouts of structural elements are examined and evaluated to formulate the most efficient and affordable plan. The correct furnace and AC unit is recommended matching the application to make your indoor air environment cooled and heated for continual comfort in all four seasons.

In addition to air conditioner or furnace repair and installation, Young & Sons Heating and AC also installs air source heat pumps, indoor air quality improvement appliances such as American Standard's AccuClean air cleanersJim of the Young & Sons Heating and A/C team reads architectural drawings for a home heating project., and radiant in floor heat featuring Uponor radiant products. For our customers who have hot water heat, we offer Samsung Mini-Split Ductless Air Conditioning equipment for quiet energy efficient operation. Young & Sons serves the St. Croix River Valley of Minnesota including Stillwater, Forest Lake, Scandia, Hugo, Mahtomedi, White Bear Lake, Bayport, Lakeland, Oak Park Heights, Afton, Lake Elmo, and Marine on St. Croix in the eastern Twin Cities towns and suburbs.

For more than 20 years, Young & Sons Heating and Air Conditioning has been helping folks throughout the St. Croix Valley area of Minnesota enjoy total indoor air comfort in their homes and businesses providing a consistent temperature controlled environment. Our team of experts will take care of you.
